What we're about

Four lads who grew up in and around the Waitakere ranges decided that the Auckland rock scene was in need of some earthy brightness.

In January 2016, Adrian Hooke, Anton Boven, Luka Zubcic, and Nick Maddren started developing their new take on an ancient sound.
